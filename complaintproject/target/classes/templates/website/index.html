How It Works

Our Consumer Complaint Portal provides an easy way to lodge complaints and track resolutions. Whether you are facing issues with a product or service, we are here to assist you in resolving them efficiently.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fast and reliable complaint handling.

Steps to File a Complaint

  1. Register on our portal with your details.
  2. Provide the necessary information about your issue.
  3. Submit your complaint and receive a tracking ID.
  4. Our team will work with the concerned authorities to resolve the issue.
